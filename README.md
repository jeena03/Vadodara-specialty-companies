# Company Research and ICP Analysis

Prepared by: Jeena Anandita  
Date: 4 May 2026  
Universe: 25 Companies  

---

## Overview

This project focuses on identifying and evaluating companies located in Vadodara based on a defined Ideal Customer Profile (ICP). The goal was to build a curated and reliable list of companies that genuinely fit the required business criteria.

Instead of collecting random names, each company has been carefully researched and assessed to ensure it aligns with the expected profile.

---

## What This Project Contains

The repository includes a structured dataset of 25 Vadodara-based companies with:

- company name and basic details  
- business segment and product focus  
- decision-maker information  
- evaluation across six key criteria  
- one-line evidence supporting each score  
- final qualification verdict  

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Each company has been assessed across the following six areas:

- C1: Whether the company is a manufacturer  
- C2: Presence and operations in India  
- C3: Level of differentiation in products or processes  
- C4: Technical strength of the decision-maker  
- C5: Whether the company operates in a growing sector  
- C6: Signs of active growth or business momentum  

The purpose of this evaluation is to identify companies that are not only relevant but also show long-term potential.

---

## Outcome

The final result is a clean and structured dataset of Vadodara-based companies that are:

- relevant to the ICP  
- properly verified  
- supported by clear reasoning and evidence  

This ensures that the output is not just a list, but a meaningful and usable set of companies.

---

## Conclusion

This project reflects a practical approach to company research within a specific industrial cluster. The focus was on accuracy, relevance, and structured evaluation to identify high-quality companies in Vadodara.
